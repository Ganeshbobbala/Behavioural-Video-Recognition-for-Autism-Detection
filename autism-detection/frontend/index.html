Methodology

Our system combines computer vision and deep learning to analyze behavioral patterns with precision.

1

Video Upload

Upload a recorded video of a child for behavioral analysis. Supports common formats like MP4, AVI, and MOV.

2

AI Processing

Frames are extracted and analyzed using MediaPipe for body/hand landmarks and facial key points in real-time.

3

Pattern Detection

Deep learning models (CNN + LSTM) identify behaviors: hand flapping, repetitive movements, and eye contact avoidance.

4

Results & Insights

View confidence levels and detailed reports for each detected behavioral pattern with timeline-based mapping.

Video Capture Instructions

Follow these steps to ensure the highest accuracy for our AI models.

Lighting

Ensure the room is well-lit. Avoid shadows across the face or body.

Full Body View

The child's entire body should be visible in the frame for movement detection.

Duration

Record at least 30-60 seconds of natural interaction or play.
